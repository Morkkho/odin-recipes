Odin Recipes

Ah, food! The one thing no creature on the planet can do without. It fills us up, nourishes our brains, gives us the strength we need to survive and go on about our days of giving each other hell. We would eat as much as we can if our stomachs were not so restricted. Without it, the chain of life's existence would be lost; the chain of life's existence that is in itself the food chain.

In a food chain, there exists a variety of delicacies. That consumed by our kind, however, is vast and of a rich diversity that the rest of the creatures would be envious of us if they were as greedy and imperial as we - a tiny constituent of the planet - are. Anyway, since that greed in particular isn't going anywhere ever - well, unless nature takes care of us as it has with a lot of creatures in time - we might as well make the most of it. Let's look, therefore, at some of international dishes that often incite that greed in us, shall we.
