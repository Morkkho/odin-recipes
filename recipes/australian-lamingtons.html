Australian Lamingtons

There is uncertainty as to the origing of Lamingtons. Named after Lord and Lady Lamington of Queensland, this special desert is generally known to have originated from Australia. However, some say it was created in New Zealand. Regardless of its origin, this square spongy cake coated in dark chocolate icing and tossed about in tossed about in dessicated coconut has now been adopted the world over, particularly in Croatia where it is known as Cupavci.

Ingredients

Ingredients for chocolate icing and Coconut
